Isabelle

Truly, the absolute best of the worst, Isabelle, for some reason, remains my all-time favorite character to play. Her ability to zone is nearly unrivaled (in my opinion), and she has one of my favorite tools ever. It's called Lloid Mine, and he's the best thing the game has to offer. Sure, she struggles to close out stocks, but she also slows the game to a crawl, and has confirms that *can* claim stocks if they work out.

Isabelle is my favorite character in the game bar none. Every single thing about playing her, I enjoy, regardless of the fact that she's very much a low tier, like the other two.
