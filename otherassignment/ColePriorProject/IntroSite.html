A Journey Through the Mains of Cole "Brightheart" Bracken

Super Smash Bros Ultimate is quite possibly my most played game, and I've gone through some phases when picking characters.
So, for the sake of posterity, I've decided to use this project to record this journey, for my sake, if nobody else's.


The first order of business, of course, is to figure out what makes a character fun for me.

  • Slow gameplan
  • Ability to force interaction
  • Uncommon character
  • Projectiles and game stall

The above characteristics are the keymark of my personal favorite types of characters. In game terms, these characters would typically be considered either "zoners" or "trappers," depending on other factors. I find that I enjoy each archetype pretty evenly however, meaning my time in Ult has been a journey through most of them. This'll be focusing on my main three.
