The Holder of the Triforce of Wisdom

Zelda

In my opinion, one of the most fun characters in the game, Zelda may not be as durable as say, the King, but she makes up for that by being harder to combo. In addition, her gameplan, though similar to the King's, is much safer. She boasts multiple projectiles, a reliable "get off me option," and much more consistent ways to close out stocks.

Hyrule's princess might just be my second favorite character in the game, barely falling short of the one character remaining on my list.
